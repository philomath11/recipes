Chocolate Brownies

The BEST brownie recipe! With crispy edges, fudgy middles, and rich chocolate flavor, these homemade brownies will disappear in no time.

Homemade Brownie Recipe Ingredients:-

  • 250gm all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 500 gm white sugar
  • 50 gm cocoa powder
  • 3 eggs
  • 100 gm butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • walnuts and almonds

Recepie instructions:-

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease the 9x13 inch pan.
  2. Mix sugar, flour, eggs, cocoa powder, melted butter, essence, baking powder in a large bowl until the batter is combined and smooth. Spread the batter into the prepared pan and decorate it with almonds/walnuts.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven untill top is dry and edges have started to pull away from the sides of the pan, about 20-30 minutes. Cool before slicing into squares.
  4. Your delicious brownies are ready. Enjoy!!!
